The Runaway Bride

updated 11.3.16

This Random Event is located at a church just near Sandy Shores and the Zancudo Fort. A woman dressed in a bridal gown will scream for help, so stop near the church and give her a ride.

advertisement

The runaway bride will ask to be taken back to Vinewood, only to have the groom come after you in a Patriot vehicle! Evading the groom will need a tricky maneuver.

As soon as the groom starts slamming into your car, STOP THE CAR. Rotate the camera to see the groom stop his car and then get out to confront you. As soon as he gets out of his car, FLEE!

When the coast is clear, drive the bride to her house at Vinewood to complete the Random Event. Unfortunately, there is no consolation prize.

Random Event Complete!

MISSION NOTE: ALTRUIST ACOLYTE

If playing as Trevor, you can deliver the bride to the Altruist Cult near Mount Chiliad, earning you $5,000, as well as the "Altruist Acolyte" trophy/achievement.
