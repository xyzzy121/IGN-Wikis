Fallout 4 Wiki Guide

Concrete

Top Contributors: Brendan Graeber, Michael Koczwara, Jared Petty + more
Last Edited: 3 Nov 2016 6:10 pm
FlagView History

Concrete is one of many Components which can be obtained or created by scrapping specific Items. This component is an essential part of Crafting, as well as an important element to maintaining a settlement!

You can make a variety of items and structures in a settlement with Concrete. If you find yourself needing more reserves of Concrete, try breaking down junk you find in the wasteland. You can also purchase shipments of Concrete from certain vendors in marketplaces or roaming certain areas.
