Fiber

updated 4.11.17

Fiber is one of the first basic resources in ARK that is a main ingredient in many recipes throughout the game. 

advertisement

Fiber can be collected by hand, metal sickle, Dire Bear, Gigantopithecus, and/or a Therizinosaurus by harvesting plants.
