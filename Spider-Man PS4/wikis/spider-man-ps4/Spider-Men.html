Spider-Men

updated 12.31.18

This page contains information on one of the Side Missions - Spider-Men - which will include main objectives, side objectives, and any points of interest along the way.

  • Location: Harlem
  • Available: Complete The Mask Main Mission
  • Rewards: 1,500 XP
advertisement

After completing The Mask, you can find a new mission available up in Harlem, not far from the local Police Department. Find a store owner on the street corner who wants to thank you for saving his daughter...but that wasnt you?

Go to the Theater

Video Guide: Spider-Men

The place in question isnt far from the deli - its just a bit Northwest of your location near one of the bridges out of the city. Look for the June Kraus Theatre sign, and the police barricade, and check the place out. The thugs arent dead (thankfully), and the policeman missed the action, but the daughter in question is still there - and she seems to think you were checking out a fire!

Head down South again until you come to an intersection surrounded by fire trucks and officers, with a still smouldering side of the building. The firefighters will applaud your bravery, as will a woman whose friend you saved. You can also investigate the fire escape to see how he got up and down, and an extinguisher around the corner with a unique suppressant.

Note: In Side Missions, Spectrograph Puzzles can vary in difficulty depending on how far you are in the main story. The solution below indicates a puzzle from as soon as the mission becomes available, but the puzzle you encounter may be more complex!

If you remember the Spectrograph puzzles, youll need to use it to match the patterns - as two combining patterns can form thicker results. The left pattern has two normal lines in the middle and a thick line on the right, using the bottom fragment and the lone line fragment two above it. The right pattern has two thick lines with a smaller line in the middle, and uses the first and third fragment in your inventory.

Follow the Chemical Trail

With the chemical agent the fake Spider-Man used, you can follow a new blue trailer from the dumpster down an alley and along the top of Golden Gate Park, before doubling back North. You can even check in at a hot dog stand at a nearby intersection to find out your imposter got a free hot dog! That jerk!

Up ahead, a policeman has a thug in custody, and he may have had a run in with your doppelganger. Hell talk about the drug deal on the roof, prompting you to visit and admire your doubles handiwork. Investigate the beaten thugs, and finally the drugs that were left behind. When you check out the other side of the building, youll find your double finally biting off more than he can chew.

Quickly disarm the henchmen by aiming and tapping R1 when you hone in on the gun, then get to punching. Your fellow Spider-Man will help out, and hes no slouch - even if he isnt bullet-resistant. Speaking of which, more of Fisks thugs will appear with guns, so its up to you to take them out. Help out by webbing or using gadgets like Impact Web to stick them to walls so they cant harass you.

After the next wave, a few cars will show up with more gun-toting thugs, and more will appear on the upper levels to snipe at you, as will a few with rocket launchers. Fake Spider-Man will wisely bow out of this fight, letting you web the thugs before they can do too much damage. Remember that a webbed up enemy can be knocked into walls and will stick there, if you happen to run out of Impact Webs or other gadgets.

After the fight, Fake Spider-Man will have learned his lesson, and will wisely bow out of the costumed justice scene, and the mission will be complete.
