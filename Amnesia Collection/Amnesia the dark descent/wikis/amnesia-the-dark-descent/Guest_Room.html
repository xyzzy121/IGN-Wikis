Guest Room

Top Contributors: Lone_Wolfe70, Nixice, Bonweth + more
Last Edited: 9 Nov 2021 2:21 am
  • Flag
  • View History

Welcome to probably the smallest area in the game!

Immediately after entering, Daniel will receive a flashback of himself trying to find a journal. After the flashback, go to the back right corner and pick up the Oil on the shelf at about eye-level. While you're here, try and open the door next to you. Daniel will write a Memento noting that the lock is broken and to find another way to open the door. Now, go over to the desk near the window and pick up Daniel's Diary - Revelations (1/3). In this same desk desk, be sure to pick up the Laudanum in the middle drawer. Now, head over to the bedroom.

advertisement

Upon entering, get the Tinderbox sitting on top of the fireplace. As something optional, if you head over to the bed and interact with it, Daniel will receive a flashback of his recurring nightmares. Next to the desk in the room, grab Daniel's Diary - Revelations (2/3) on top of the small table. As for the desk itself, grab the Crowbar laying on top of it. Also be sure to grab the Tinderbox in the small cabinet of this desk. Now, head back to the locked door and use the crowbar on it. To pry open the door, grab the crowbar and pull it to the right. Doing so will net you a sanity boost and a brief flashback about a key.

Note: Stay away from the right side of this room. You will spring another surprise...

Once in this small room, Daniel will write a Memento noting that a key is hidden somewhere in the area. Before searching, pick up Daniel's Diary - Revelations (3/3) on the dresser straight ahead. As for the key, look on the left wall. You should see a painting that will swing and temporarily reveal empty space behind it. Grab the painting off the wall to receive a flashback of Daniel hiding it in the first place. To grab the key itself, pick up the bottle it's encased in and throw it against the wall. Doing so will net you a sanity boost, and you can now retrieve the Machine Room Key.

Warning: If you approach the wardrobe on the right side of the room, a Grunt will spawn. If this happens, close the door to the room and hide in the wardrobe itself until the Grunt leaves.

With the key in hand, head back to the Back Hall. Head left and enter the other remaining room on this floor: the Study.
