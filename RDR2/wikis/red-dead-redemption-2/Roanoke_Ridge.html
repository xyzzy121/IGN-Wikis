Roanoke Ridge

Top Contributors: Angie Harvey, Casey DeFreitas, Mudkip1430 + more
Last Edited: 2 Jan 2019 11:23 pm
FlagView History

Roanoke Ridge is a region in Red Dead Redemption 2’s state of New Hanover. Although Roanoke Ridge is a primarily wooded region that has a heavy focus on the coal mining industry, it does begin to transition towards more swamp-like conditions the closer you get to the Lemoyne border in the Southern area of the region.
